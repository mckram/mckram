who am i?

Defining oneself is a rather daunting task. Are we a collection of our hobbies, goals, dreams and achievements? Are we an ever-changing confederation of ideas, experiences and beliefs? Or are we some combination of the two? Who can say, certainly not me. I try not to define myself often, in my opinion, to define is to limit. We try to categorize the world and ourselves in order to make sense of this hodgepodge of a life. But in doing so we miss so much of the picture. At the risk of wasting time on useless details, I want to capture the full story.

In trying to find myself, I make lists of things that I enjoy. Unfortunately I end up with messy collections of words like the following...I like drawing, analyzing data, helping people, being nice, listening to sad music, petting cats, collecting acorns, finding four leaf clovers, making things pretty, running in the rain, getting hyperfocused on specific tasks, sharing my opinions etc etc. I care deeply about mother earth and all sentient beings. I would like to find some way to contribute positively to the world. I’m still searching for a way to accomplish this goal…wish me luck!! :)
